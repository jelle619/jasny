Jasny

Clean up junk on your desktop by organising files in their appropriate folder - automatically.

Download for macOS
Jasny can handle a wide array of file types

Applications

Automatically moved to the Applications folder where they will easily be accesible from the Launchpad.

Documents

Most text documents fall under this catagory. These will automatically be moved to the Documents folder.

Pictures

The most popular file formats for pictures, like JPEG and PNG, will be automatically moved to the Pictures folder.

Music

Music and other audio files will be moved to the Music folder. This makes importing them into apps like iTunes easier.

Movies

Movies and videos will be moved to the Movies folder. It will be easier to find them and your popcorn won't get cold.
